Completed Items

The following requested updates have been completed and are ready for review.

Completed Work

  1. Comma formatting was added for number fields to make millions, prices, and month values easier to read.
  2. The Special page is visible to all users. Only admin and sub-admin users can edit Special units.
  3. Newly created units now appear faster in unit pages after creation.
  4. A smart paste field was added to create units faster by filling unit details from pasted information.
  5. Special units now include a button that generates ready-to-post copy for Facebook and social media.
  6. The thumbnail image frame and “confidential” text were removed from generated PDFs.
  7. Generated PDFs now show a maximum of two images per page in the image grid.
  8. Total amount is no longer adjustable by users.
  9. Installments are applied automatically on the first day of the month.
  10. A day value was added to the installment timetable to allow easier testing and control.
  11. The back button now moves one step back instead of jumping too far back.
  12. The light and dark theme toggle was adjusted to reduce screen flashing.
  13. Login behavior was improved so users can get into the app faster.
  14. Sales representatives cannot edit restricted unit controls or management-only actions.
  15. Paid amount was removed. Down payment is now treated as the paid amount and updates when installments are paid.
  16. Sales users cannot mark units as sold, on hold, or change unit status. They can still generate unit PDFs.
  17. The manual “mark as paid” feature was removed from installments.
  18. The app no longer signs users in automatically after they log out.
